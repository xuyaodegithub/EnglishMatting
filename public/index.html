How the artificial intelligence cutout / background removal works?

PicUP.AI uses the most advanced artificial intelligence deep learning technology-Deep Convolutional Neural Networks, adopts a multi-stage high-throughput automatic encoding infrastructure for image semantic segmentation, and adds foreground objects that predict the image transparent channel (Alpha Matting). Image semantic segmentation is the automatic segmentation of the object area from the image by the machine and the recognition of its content. When processing an image, it is down to the pixel level, that is, the method assigns each pixel in the image to an object category. In this way, artificial intelligence can identify the subject and the background, so that they can be separated.

Q

Is the technique of online portrait matting particularly difficult?

Yes, everyone who has used PhotoShop knows it well, especially with flying hair, or when the colors of the background and human skin and/or clothing accessories are very similar, or there is narrow background between the arms and legs, etc. It is tedious and time consuming for doing it well manually. Common matting methods will assign each pixel in the image to a certain object category, either black or white. However, because the portrait has hair, if you want to perform matting of the portrait, you need to remove the background and retain the translucent feeling of the hair, so that it will be more natural. That way, in the case of subsequent addition/change of background, change of color, it will blend better, rather than the feeling of a steel plate. In addition, the human body and clothing are flexible items, not like sofa chairs, and which will not deform. The posture of the human body is ever-changing, standing, sitting, cross-legged, leaning back, leaning down, characters overlapping, covering, back, side, not to mention difficult postures such as dancing and raising legs. The wearings of the human body, long sleeves, short sleeves, long skirts, short skirts, wedding dresses, headscarves, scarves, etc. makes it difficult to judge the outline of the body and clothes. Transparent hair, ever-changing postures, and flexible clothing all add dozens of times more difficulty to portrait cutouts. Because it is particularly difficult, the technological breakthrough in the field of portrait matting is also the key advantage of PicUP.AI technology.
